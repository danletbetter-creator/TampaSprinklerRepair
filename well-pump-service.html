Common Questions

Well Pump FAQs

How long do well pumps typically last?

Most well pumps last 15-25 years depending on use and maintenance. Regular service extends lifespan significantly.

What are signs my pump needs repair?

Low water pressure, strange noises, inability to start, or inconsistent flow are signs of pump problems. Call immediately if you notice these.

Can you service lake-drawn pumps?

Yes! We specialize in lake-drawn irrigation systems and surface pump maintenance for Florida properties.

How much does well pump replacement cost?

Submersible pump replacement typically costs $1,500-3,500. Surface pumps may cost $2,000-4,000. We provide estimates for your specific system.

Do you offer maintenance plans?

Yes! Regular maintenance prevents problems and extends pump life. We offer seasonal inspections and maintenance packages.

What about fountain pump maintenance?

We service ornamental and fountain pumps as well, ensuring your water features operate beautifully year-round.
