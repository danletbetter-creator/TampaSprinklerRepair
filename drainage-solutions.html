Common Questions

Drainage FAQs

What's the difference between French drains and catch basins?

French drains redirect subsurface water through a sloped gravel bed and perforated pipe. Catch basins collect surface water and direct it away from the property. We often use both for complete protection.

How much do drainage systems cost?

Cost varies based on the size of your property and the type of system needed. French drains typically cost $1,000-3,000, while complete systems can be $3,000-8,000+. We provide free estimates.

How long do drainage systems last?

Professionally installed systems last 20+ years. Proper maintenance and occasional cleaning of drain pipes ensures maximum lifespan.

Can you help with my flooded yard?

Absolutely! We can diagnose the flooding issue and provide a solution whether it's a French drain, sump pump, or catch basin system.

Do you need permits for drainage work?

In most cases, yes. We handle all necessary permits and inspections to ensure your system meets local codes.

Will drainage work damage my landscaping?

We minimize landscape disruption and restore your property after installation. We'll work around existing plants and features when possible.
