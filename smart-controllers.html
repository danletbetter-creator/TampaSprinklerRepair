Common Questions

Smart Controller FAQs

Are smart controllers compatible with my system?

Most modern systems are compatible. We assess your current controller and install the right smart upgrade for your setup.

How much does a smart controller cost?

Controllers range from $300-800 depending on features and zones. Professional installation adds $200-500. We provide free estimates.

Do I need WiFi for a smart controller?

Yes, WiFi is required for remote control and weather-based adjustments. Most work with standard home WiFi networks.

How much water can I save?

Typical savings are 20-50% depending on your current watering habits. Smart scheduling and weather adaptation add up quickly.

What if my WiFi goes down?

The controller continues running on its stored schedule. You just can't adjust it remotely until WiFi is restored.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes! We provide technical support and can help optimize your schedules seasonally to maximize water savings.
