Common Questions

Irrigation Design FAQs

How long does a new irrigation system last?

A professionally designed and installed system typically lasts 15-20 years with proper maintenance. Quality components and expert installation ensure durability in Tampa's climate.

What's included in the design service?

Our design includes site assessment, CAD drawings, zone planning, component selection, permitting, and installation supervision to ensure your system performs optimally.

Will my system meet water conservation requirements?

Yes! We design all systems to exceed Florida Water Star standards and local water conservation requirements, helping you save money while protecting our environment.

Can you design a system for an existing landscape?

Absolutely. We specialize in retrofitting existing landscapes with new irrigation systems customized to your plant types, soil, and available water sources.

How much does a new system cost?

Cost varies based on landscape size, complexity, and components. We provide free estimates after assessing your property. Most residential systems range from $3,000 to $10,000.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we work with several financing partners to make new irrigation systems affordable. Ask us about payment plans during your free estimate.
